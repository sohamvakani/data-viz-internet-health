Does Internet Access Drive Better Health Outcomes? A Global Look at 176 Countries

By Soham Vakani

Data Year: 2022 | Countries: 176

Data source: Our World in Data

As internet access expands across the globe, so does its potential to transform how people access healthcare information, connect with services, and improve their quality of life. This visualization explores whether countries with greater internet connectivity also tend to have better health outcomes. Explore the data across 176 countries to see the patterns for yourself.
